Sales Order Tracker

Sales Order Tracking is a critical component of supply chain management that enables businesses to monitor the status and progress of customer orders from placement through delivery. Each sales order can contain multiple shipments to clients, with each shipment having a specific type that categorizes its purpose and handling requirements. Effective tracking systems provide real-time visibility into order fulfillment, inventory allocation, and logistics operations, allowing teams to identify bottlenecks and respond quickly to delays. Modern order trackers integrate data from multiple systems including inventory management, warehouse operations, and shipping logistics to create a unified view of each order's journey and its associated shipments.
